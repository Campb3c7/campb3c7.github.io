A Card Game

By Colin Campbell

In this game you are an unlucky assasin hired to rid the world of evil. Every turn you can sneak up and attack the enemy, however the enemy will figure you out, and will attack every certain number of turns. Also beware that bad things will happen before every turn. You may buy better cards from the shop at any time. They go straight into your discard Pile. Your goal is to rid the world of all it's evil. Good Luck.
